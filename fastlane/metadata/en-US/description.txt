FoodTracks is the smart app designed specifically for food trucks and mobile food businesses. Powered by artificial intelligence, FoodTracks transforms how you manage your business with powerful inventory management, sales tracking, and demand prediction tools.

SMART INVENTORY MANAGEMENT
- Track your inventory in real time with a simple, intuitive interface
- Scan your products and receipts using your phone's camera
- Get automatic alerts when stock is running low
- Manage your suppliers and orders with ease

AI-POWERED PREDICTIONS
- Anticipate demand with our artificial intelligence algorithms
- Optimize your purchases to reduce food waste
- Analyze sales trends by location, day, and weather
- Make data-driven decisions with confidence
