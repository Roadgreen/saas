FoodTracks is an inventory management and sales prediction app for food truck operators.

To test the app:
1. Create an account using any email address
2. Navigate through the dashboard to see sales analytics
3. Go to the Inventory section to manage stock items
4. Check the Predictions tab to see AI-powered demand forecasts

The app uses location services to detect the user's selling location (GPS).
The app uses the camera for scanning products and receipts.

No special hardware is required for testing.

For PRO subscription testing, you can use a sandbox Apple ID.
